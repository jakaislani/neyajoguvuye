Are marigolds safe for pets?

published on August 18, 2024
Toxicity to pets Marigolds (Tagetes species) may cause mild irritation to the gastrointestinal tract when ingested. The sap from the plant may also cause irritation to the skin if dermal exposure occurs. Are marigolds safe for cats and dogs? Marigolds are mildly poisonous to dogs and cats, although symptoms usually clear up within an hour of ingestion. The ASCPA defines Marigolds as non-toxic to cats and dogs.
