Ari Fletcher: Unknown Facts About Her

Ari Fletcher’s fame majorly comes from being the girlfriend of Moneybagg Yo and previous high-profile relationships. However, she holds more to her name than that. She is also a social media influencer and entrepreneur who owns the Kyche Extension hair company.

Ari Fletcher is From Chicago, Illinois

It was on July 12, 1995, that Ariana Fletcher was born to her parents. From our research, the 28 years old grew up in Chicago, Illinois alongside her siblings Kyle Jamison and Ashey. As for her ethnicity, she revealed on Twitter that she’s a Creole and Purti Rican this came after she uploaded a picture that got people arguing about her nationality.

We also garnered that she was majorly raised by her mother, Erin Flecher who was a waitress at a local cafe. Not so much has been established about her father, however, it is reported in many quarters that he was a high school janitor and wasn’t so close to his daughter.

She Dropped Out of College after her Brother’s Death

Ari Fletcher graduated from Chicago Vocational High School and later enrolled at Northern Illinois University in Dekalb, Chicago. Unfortunately, she dropped out of school in the first semester following the death of her brother who died in 2013 after battling an illness. As you would find, Ari shared a very close relationship with her brother and thusly, the news of his death left her devastated.

Ari also had to drop out to support her family. She took a bartender job at Adrianna’s nightclub and also juggled other odd jobs like working in the spa and salon.

To pay tribute to him, she drew a tattoo of his face on her shoulder and also named her major social media pages after him. She noted that this way his name will never be forgotten. She also mentioned that anyone who needs to talk to her must first talk about him.

Ari Fletcher is a Model, Entrepreneur, and Social Media Influencer

A few years after Ari left odd jobs, she started modeling on Instagram to make ends meet thanks to her impressive physique. She quickly earned a lot of followers and promoted several brands including Charette Cosmetics, Savage x Fenty, Jodore Fancy, Moya’s Majesty, Fashion Nova, and Beyani Hair Collection.

In line with her high-profile relationships, she has since amassed millions of followers on several social media platforms including Instagram, Twitter, and Youtube. According to an interview, she revealed that she makes at least $150,000 on Instagram, and over time, it may have doubled.

As an entrepreneur, she founded her hair brand “Kyche Extensions” in 2016. She noted that she was raised around business-minded people and it has always been a passion to have a running business. She also said that she has several projects she hopes to establish in the future.

A Look at Her Relationship Profile 

In previous years, Ari has made headlines not just for her career successes but also for her relationships with celebrities. She has dated the likes of Freeband Joey, Herbert Randall Wright III, Gervonta Tank Davis, and Antonio Blakeney.

Freeband Joey

Ari and Freeband dated from 2012 to 2015. The exact time their paths crossed isn’t known, however, sources have it that they started sometime in June 2012. They maintained an excellent relationship for over 3 years before parting ways. What caused their breakup isn’t known but sources say they were on and off before officially breaking up.

Herbert Randall Wright III (G Herbo)

The social media influencer Ari and Herbert Randall famed as G Herbo dated from 2015 to 2018. However, their relationship was riddled with a lot of problems linked to infidelity and battery on G Herbo’s end. He allegedly cheated on her with Tania Williams. According to the story, Ari was going through postpartum depression at the time.

Even though these claims were denied by Herbert, their relationship had already become irrevocably so broken. Nonetheless, they are raising their son together. Needless to say, the pair’s relationship saw the birth of their son, Yosohn Santana.

Gervota Tank Davis 

Ari and the professional boxer, Gervonta started dating a few months after she had parted ways with G Herbo. The relationship between the two was highly publicized. However, it ended as soon as it had started. This was one of the messy breakups Ari has recorded.

From what we garnered, Gervonta reportedly cheated on her with a striper. Aside from that, they had also gone physical.

Katorah Hortense Marrero (Young M.A)

The relationship between Ari and Young M.A. lasted for only a year from 2018 to 2019. The two identify as LGBT members and their relationship was widely known. It was also around this time that she came out as bisexual in a podcast known as “Lip Service”

Antonio Blakeney

In May 2019, the news made waves that Ari and Antonio were in a relationship and this was other confirmed by Ari who had visited the pro boxer in Miami while he was training during his off-season. Not so much is known about their relationship as it reportedly ended within a few months.

Who is Ari Flecher Dating Now?

At the moment, the social media influencer and entrepreneur is in a relationship with  Moneybagg Yo. Details on how they crossed paths have not been established however, the relationship between the two dates back to October 2019. Ari later confirmed their relationship in a 2020 interview.

Though their relationship has seen several difficult moments, the pair have maintained a great relationship throughout the years. They were once rumored to have parted ways after a video of Ari tweaking with an unknown man went viral. However, Ari explained that he was just a friend, and her boyfriend, Moneybagg asked the public not to worry about him as he doesn’t have trust issues.

They had even unfollowed each other on Instagram on two occasions which sparked breakup rumors. Nonetheless, the news was debunked by Moneybagg who said “We 4eva Locked In”.

Though their relationship appears to be on and off, the pair are still together and nothing suggests otherwise at the moment.
