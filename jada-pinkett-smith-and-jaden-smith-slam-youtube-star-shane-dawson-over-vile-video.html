Jada Pinkett Smith and Jaden Smith slam YouTube star Shane Dawson over vile video

Willow Smith and Jada Pinkett Smith. Photo / Getty

Jada Pinkett Smith and Jaden Smith have tweeted their disgust at a YouTube star after his vile video about Willow Smith resurfaced online.

The video clip shows YouTuber Shane Dawson pretending to masturbate over a poster of Willow Smith, who was just 11 at the time.

Willow's brother, Jaden, tweeted on the weekend that the clip was "not okay".

"I would never talk about a child in any way that was inappropriate," he said. "That is disgusting. That is gross. That is something I did for shock value. I promise that is not me."

"I've had a lot of pain, I've had a lot of issues with my family," he said. "I took that pain, and I turned it into jokes. Instead of joking about them, I should've gone to therapy."
