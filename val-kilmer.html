Val Kilmer or Val Edward Kilmer is an American who is popularly known for his acting skills. As of 2022, Val Kilmer’s net worth is $10 million. Kilmer was actually a stage actor who finally got renowned and earned fame with his appearance in comedy films. Some of his films are Top Secret! And Real Genius. He was also seen in a military action film titled Top Gun. Willow was his first fantasy film.

Kilmer is mostly known for starring in the characters of real-life figures in movies and the most notable ones are The Doors, where he was seen as Jim Morrison. In True Romance, he was seen as an apparition of Elvis Presley, in Tombstone, he appeared as Doc Holliday, in The Ghost and the Darkness, Kilmer was seen playing the character of John H. Patterson.

Furthermore, he was also seen as John Holmes in Wonderland and Philip II of Macedon in Alexander. Some of his famous portrayals are seen in Batman Forever as Bruce Wayne/Batman, in The Saint as Simon Templar and The Prince of Egypt in Moses.

He was also nominated for Saturn Award for Best Supporting Actor his impressive performances owing to Heat as Chris, in the year 1995 and in Kiss Kiss Bang Bang as “Gay Perry”, in the year 2005.

Kilmer is not just an actor, but he also wrote a book titled I’m Your Huckleberry: A Memoir. And, he is also a supporter of Native American affairs. Other than this, Kilmer is an advocate of environmental protection.

Val Kilmer Wiki/Biography

Born on 31 December 1959, Val Kilmer’s age is 62 years as of 2022. He was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. His present residence is in Malibu, California, United States. Kilmer is of Swedish descent on his maternal side. And, on his parental side, he has German, English, Welsh, Scots-Irish/Northern Irish, and French Huguenot descent. He is an American by nationality and followed Christian Science as a religion.
