Camidoh Reacts As His Sugarcane Remix Music Video Hits 20 Million Views On YouTube In 2 Month

Afrobeats singing sensation, Raphael Camidoh Kofi Attachie, better known by his stage name, Camidoh on Friday, April 8, 2022 served his listeners with the remix to his already heard tune, “Sugarcane”.

Produced by Phantom, ‘Sugarcane Remix’ had vocal assistance from renowned Ghanaian singer and Legacy Life Entertainment star, King Promise alongside buzzing Nigerian atcs, Mayorkun and Darkoo.

The beautifully crafted song during its first week of release debuted on Apple Music Top 100 in several countries across the globe and has also amassed impressive numbers on all the major streaming platforms.

As a follow up, Camidoh dropped visuals for ‘Sugarcane Remix’ which was directed and shot by popular Ghanaian cinematographer, Rex. The video was shot in different and beautiful locations in Ghana and Nigeria.

Well, the colorful and eye pleasing video to the chart topping tune which was released on Tuesday, June 7, 2022 has surpassed 20 million views on YouTube within 2 months. Camidoh has reacted to this feat. See screenshot of his reaction below.
