About

Lifestyle blogger who is known as the founder and proprietor of the website IvoryLane.com. She has also gained a sizable social media following, with her personal Instagram account having amassed more than 350,000 followers.

Before Fame

She originally began a blog to document her new family's day-to-day lives. She began Ivory Lane as a fashion and lifestyle blog after being repeatedly asked what she was wearing in her family blog posts.
