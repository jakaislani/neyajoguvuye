Omarion Wiki Biography

Omari Ishmael Grandberry, to the public known by a stage name of Omarion, is a famous American actor, singer, dancer, as well as a songwriter. Omarion is perhaps best known as a member of a music group called “B2K”, which consisted of Lil’ Fizz, J-Boog, Omarion and Raz-B. The group was active from 1998 until 2004, and during this time managed to release 7 studio albums and make an appearance in Chris Stokes’ film entitled “You Got Served” with Meagan Good, Marques Houston and Robert Hoffman. “B2K” first topped the charts in 2001 with the release of a single “Uh Huh”. “Uh Huh” was a mixture of dance beats and unique delivery, which set them apart from other boy bands. Shortly afterwards, the group released their debut album under the name of “B2K”, which landed on a #2 spot on the Billboard 200 chart and sold over 109 000 copies during its debut week. Following the success of their debut album, the band came out with “Pandemonium!”, which featured the group’s most popular single “Bump, Bump, Bump”. Around the same time, “B2K” went on a tour with Bow Wow, Nick Cannon, Mario and other artist, and made an appearance on television screens. However, in 2004, due to many internal conflicts, the group split up and Omarion went on to launch his solo career.
