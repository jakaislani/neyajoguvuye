published on July 20, 2024
Chuck Bundrant, born in 1941 in Seattle, Washington, is the chairman and co-founder of Trident Seafoods, the largest vertically integrated seafood company in North America. He and two crab fishermen created the Bilikin, the first boat to feature crab cookers and freezing equipment onboard, and he has since popularized pollock, which was once considered a trash fish, and now sells it to major chains like Long John Silver's and McDonald's.
