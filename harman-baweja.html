Harman Baweja is one of the most renowned growing faces of Bollywood. He made his first debut with the movie Love Story 2050 that was released in the year 2008. The film was directed by Baweja’s father Harry and Mom Pammi. Harman Baweja is a proficient dancer specializing in Kathak dance. Baweja’s style is often contrasted with famous Bollywood Star Hrithik Roshan.

Harman Baweja was born in Chandigarh, India on 13th November, 1980. He belongs to a Sikh Jatt family. The star sign of Baweja is Scorpio. He completed his studies in Mumbai. He started his schooling at Jamnabai Narsee School and completed his education at Kishore Namit Acting Institute. After successfully completing his intermediary education, he attended University of California, Los Angeles. Harman loved his grandfather so much that in the year 2009, he amended his name as Harman S. Baweja with the intention of honoring his grandfather.

The very first movie of Harman Baweja was a romantic film Love Story 2050. In 2009, he also appeared in the films like Victory, What’s your Raashee in a leading role. He was offered a small role in Gori Tere Pyar Mein and presently he will be starred in films of Anees Bazmee- It’s My Life and also in the first movie of Raj Kundra productions and Shilpa Shetty Kundra – Dishkiyyaaoon. Both of these movies are imminent movies of Harman Baweja. He will be starred in a number of movies in future as he is the emerging star of Bollywood.

For the movie Love Story 2050, Harman Baweja was elected for Screen Awards for the most Promising Newcomer male. He was also elected for Stardust Awards for Superstar of Tomorrow for the same film.
