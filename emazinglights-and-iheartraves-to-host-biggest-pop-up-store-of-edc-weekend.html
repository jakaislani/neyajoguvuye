EmazingLights, iHeartRaves And Into The AM To Host Biggest Pop-Up Store Of EDC Weekend

Recommended Videos

Festival crowds don’t get a whole lot more rave-y than that of EDC Las Vegas, and the three-day event’s flamboyant revelers are sure to benefit from a festival attire one-stop shop. EmazingLights, iHeartRaves and Into The AM have recognized the demand, and they’re joining forces to bring the Lux Rave Superstore to the Las Vegas Strip for six full days.

In addition to selling the festival clothing and light-up apparel, the three EDM brands also have entertainment planned for their shoppers. Each day will also feature appearances from guest celebrities and festival squads Unicorn Crew and Facemelt Crew, as well as performances from a handful of DJs.

The Luxor hotel will play host to EmazingLights, iHeartRaves and Into The AM’s Lux Rave Superstore from Wednesday, June 15th through Monday, June 20th. For more information, check out the store’s official website.
