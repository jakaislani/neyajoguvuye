Louis Greatorex Biography

Louis Greatorex is a British Actor who was born and brought-up in Duffield, Derbyshire. He is widely known for his roles in the BBC1 drama series ‘Last Tango in Halifax’ as Lawrence Elliot, the BBC One tv drama series ‘The Last Post’ as Lance Corporal Paul Stoneham and in the crime drama tv series ‘Death in Paradise’ as Owen Dacre.

Louis has also appeared on notable films and series including ‘My Last Five Girlfriends’ as Giles, ‘The Legend of Dick and Dom’ as Little Dick and in ‘The Bay’ as Sam Hesketh. He is the director, writer, editor and producer of ‘Shepherds Delight’ portraying the role of Narrator.
