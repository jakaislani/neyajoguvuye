Siân Héder is an American writer and filmmaker.

Sian Heder is known for writing and directing the 2021 drama film CODA, which earned her the Academy Award for Best Adapted Screenplay and BAFTA Award for Best Adapted Screenplay, as well as a nomination for the Critics’ Choice Movie Award for Best Adapted Screenplay.

In 2010, Heder won a Peabody Award, along with her fellow writers, for her work on the acclaimed U.S. television series, Men of a Certain Age.
