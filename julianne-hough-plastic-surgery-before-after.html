Julianne Hough Plastic Surgery Before and After

Julianne Alexandra Hough was born on July 20, 1988 in Orem, Utah. She came to prominence as a professional dancer in the reality TV show Dancing with the Stars, where she became a two-time professional champion. She then ventured into acting, making her feature film debut with 2010’s Burlesque.

Her first lead role was in the 2011 remake of Footloose. She continued to have main roles in such films as Rock of Ages (2012), Safe Haven (2013) and Paradise (2013). As a singer, she released her self-titled debut album on May 20, 2008.

Has Julianne Hough had Plastic Surgery?

Some people think that the actress-singer-dancer has undergone plastic surgery to look attractive as she is now. One persistent rumor is that Hough has undergone breast implants as her bust size is believed to have doubled. Her body is still the same, except for her chest, making some people conclude that the Safe Haven star has gone under the knife for a breast augmentation. Another rumor is that of a nose job. Some people say that her nose has gotten smaller from what it used to be. If Hough really had breast implants and a nose job, the results look flawless and natural as well.
