Does Nick Cannon Need A Kidney Why Is It Trending Now On TikTok

Nick Cannon, the renowned actor, comedian, and TV host, has recently become a hot topic on TikTok due to his kidney condition. In this article, we’ll delve into who Nick Cannon is, his extraordinary family life, and why a TikTok video related to his kidney has resurfaced nearly a decade later.

Who is Nick Cannon?

Nick Cannon, a multi-talented entertainer, gained fame as a producer for the hit show “Wild ‘n Out.” However, one distinctive reason he’s known worldwide is his remarkable family life, particularly as Father’s Day approaches.

Nick Cannon’s Family:

Nick Cannon is a father to an astonishing total of 12 children, and they come from six different partners. Remarkably, nine of these children were born in just two years. Notably, Nick was previously married to the renowned singer Mariah Carey from 2008 to 201Besides his family life, he also made appearances on the popular sketch show “Real Husbands of Hollywood” alongside Kevin Hart.

The “Nick Cannon Needs A Kidney” TikTok Video:

The TikTok video related to Nick Cannon’s kidney condition has recently resurfaced, but what’s the source of this video? It originates from “Real Husbands of Hollywood,” a hilarious parody of the “Real Housewives” series. In this show, Kevin Hart, the producer, invited a camera crew to follow his life and the lives of other Hollywood husbands, although it was all fictional.

Nick Cannon’s Kidney Diagnosis:

In one memorable episode of the show, Nick Cannon reveals that he’s hospitalized due to kidney failure caused by lupus nephritis. However, his friend Kevin Hart believes Nick is merely seeking attention. Kevin even comes up with a plan to use Nick’s disorder to gain sympathy for himself.

A Hilarious Twist: In a fictionalized version of “The Doctors” show within the series, Kevin, along with cast members Boris, Nell, and Duane, gathers to determine who will donate their kidney to Nick. Each of them hides selfish motives, such as Boris wanting to promote his cult, and Kevin hoping to get closer to Mariah, Nick’s wife. Kevin goes to the extent of giving a shout-out to Mariah, stating that if anything happens to Nick, “You might lose a Cannon, but you will gain a Hart.”

The Surprise Announcement:

To everyone’s shock, the doctor announces that one of them is a perfect match to donate their kidney. As anticipation builds, the doctor reveals that it’s Kevin! The four friends erupt in joy, but Kevin realizes a bit too late that he has pledged his kidney and promptly passes out on the live show, creating a hilarious moment.

Why is it Trending on TikTok Now?

The TikTok trend surrounding Nick Cannon’s kidney condition emerged after the birth of his 12th child in December. It’s a nostalgic trend, reflecting on the humorous episode from “Real Husbands of Hollywood” and how they comically portrayed the lives of real Hollywood husbands.

Conclusion:

Nick Cannon’s kidney condition has become a TikTok sensation, resurfacing due to the show “Real Husbands of Hollywood.” This viral video offers a humorous take on a serious health issue, making people laugh and reminisce about the entertaining moments from the show.
