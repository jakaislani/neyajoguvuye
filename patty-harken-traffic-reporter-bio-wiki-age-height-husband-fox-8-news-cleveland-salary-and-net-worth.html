Patty Harken Biography

Patty Harken is an American Journalist who was born and brought up in Lakewood, California, United States of America. Currently, she works at WJW Fox 8 in Cleveland, Ohio, as a morning traffic reporter. She has been working at Fox 8 for more than 8 years since she joined the channel in January 2012.

Prior to joining Fox 8, she worked at WEWS TV5 as a Television Traffic Reporter for three years. While in WERWS TV5, she delivered to Good Morning Cleveland, the on-air traffic reports. She has also worked as a Traffic Reporter at Metro Networks in Cleveland, Ohio for 12 years.

She joined Metro Networks in 1992 and delivered traffic reports for various Cleveland radio stations such as WNCX, WDOK, WZAK, WMMS, WJNR and WLTF. While she was working there, she used different names which included her official name, Rachael Miller and Holly Copter.
