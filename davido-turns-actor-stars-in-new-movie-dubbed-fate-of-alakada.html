Davido Turns Actor Stars In New Movie Dubbed Fate Of Alakada

Singer Davido, who recently dropped his latest studio album dubbed ‘A Good Time’, has been cast in a new movie by Toyin Abraham dubbed the ‘Fate of Alakada.’

An overly exited Toyin broke the news on Instagram whiles sharing some behind the scenes photo with the serial hit maker.

She jokingly said in her post that she’s officially part of Davido’s 30 Billion Gang as they’re now working together.

She wrote: I am now officially #30BG??? Watchout for @davidoofficial in fate of Alakada ????’

Davido starred in ‘Fate Of Alakada’ along with Mercy Eke of BBNaija Pepper Dem Edition fame and other familiar Nollywood stars.
