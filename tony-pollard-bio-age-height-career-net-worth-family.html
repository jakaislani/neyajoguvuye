Tony Pollard bio, age, height, career, net worth, family

Tony Pollard is an American football running back for the Dallas Cowboys of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Memphis and was drafted by the Cowboys in the fourth round of the 2019 NFL Draft.

Tony Pollard biography

Pollard played high school football while attending Melrose High School. He started at wide receiver and cornerback for the high school football squad as a senior at both positions.

He earned over 1,200 receiving yards, 20 touchdowns, and the honour of being selected All-District 16-AAA as he assisted the club in entering the playoffs.
